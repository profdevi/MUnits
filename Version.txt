
   usage:  MUnits <conv> <value>+ [-?|-v]
           v1.1 copyright Comine.com

   Program will convert a given value from one unit type to another
   convertion type.  The -v flag will give a verbose output.

   Convertion: 

     cmha :  inv cm to Hartree         cmev :  inv cm to Electron Volts
     cmj  :  inv cm to Joules          cmk  :  inv cm to Kelvin 
     hacm :  Hartree to inv cm         haev :  Hartree to Electron Volts
     haj  :  Hartree to Joules         hak  :  Hartree to Kelvin 
     jcm  :  Joules to inv cm          jev  :  Joules to to Electron Volts
     jha  :  Joules to Hartrees        jk   :  Joules to Kelvin 
     evcm :  eV to inv cm              evj  :  Elevtron Volts to Joules
     evha :  eV to hartrees            evk  :  Elevtron Volts to Kelvin
     atfs :  atomic Unit Time to fs    fsat :  fs time to atomic unit time
     boan :  Bohr to Angstromes        anbo :  Angstromes to Bohr
     kev  :  Kelvin to eV              kj   :  Kelvin to Joules
     kcm  :  Kelvin to inv cm          kha  :  Kelvin to Hartrees
